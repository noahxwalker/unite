What
Unite Does

Centralizes the fan experience

Unite brings the key things fans check most — scores, schedules, standings, stats, and team pages — into one mobile-first experience.

Makes teams easier to follow

Users can follow schools, sports, and teams so the app feels personal instead of forcing everyone to dig through the same websites.

Gives Canadian university sports a cleaner front door

The goal is not to replace schools or conferences. The goal is to create a polished discovery layer that helps more people care about the games.
