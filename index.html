Goal
The problem

Following university sports shouldn’t feel like homework.

Scores, schedules, standings, and team information are often scattered across school pages, conference pages, social posts, and outdated layouts.

Unite is designed to cut the searching and give fans a cleaner way to keep up with the teams they care about.
