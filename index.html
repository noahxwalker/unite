Why Unite

Canadian university sports deserves a unified mobile home.

Canadian university sports already has the athletes, schools, and fans. Unite brings the experience together on mobile.

01

Information is fragmented

Scores, schedules, standings, stats, team pages, and updates are spread across school, conference, and social platforms.

02

The mobile experience is inconsistent

Fans often rely on desktop-style pages instead of a clean app-first experience built for following games.

03

Unite brings it together

One simple mobile platform helps fans follow teams, check results, and stay connected throughout the season.
