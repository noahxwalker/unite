By: Noah Walker

The
Problem

Scores, schedules, standings, stats, and team information are spread across too many places.

Information is fragmented

Scores, schedules, standings, stats, and team information are spread across school, conference, and league websites.

Mobile experience is scattered

Fans have to jump between pages, social posts, and outdated layouts just to follow a game or team.

Engagement is being left on the table

Canadian university sports has real athletes, real fans, and real stories — but the digital experience does not make it easy enough to care.

The Opportunity

Student-athletes, students, alumni, and fans need one modern place to follow Canadian university sports.

01

Centralize

One app for the core information fans are already looking for.

02

Modernize

A cleaner mobile-first experience that feels like a real sports product.

03

Grow

Make university sports easier to discover, follow, and share.

The
Solution

One modern app for scores, schedules, standings, stats, and team following.
